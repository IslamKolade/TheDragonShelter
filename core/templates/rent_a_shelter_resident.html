Rent a Shelter Resident

With so many rescues which have now been safely rehabilitated, we are running a programme which allows schools and (vetted) homes to rent one of our lovely creatures for a period of time. This programme includes our bearded dragons, African Land snails, one of our axolotl's, ball python or two of our our ever growing resident Guinea pigs. All will arrive with their own enclosure, bedding and food. We would come back weekly to check on the welfare of each animal, snakes would be fed by us but the African Land snails, beardies and Guinea pigs would be fed by their temporary owners, this gives children the chance to look after them, building interests and it's good for parents to get an insight as to whether the children's interest wains!
